<p>
Creates and new paragraph and adds whitespace between it and the last paragraph
<b>
Any information that is encased between the open and close tags will be bold.
<i>
Any information that is encased between the open and close tags will be italicized
<sup>
Any information that is encased between the open and close tags will be a superscript. EX: x4
<sub>
Any information that is encased between the open and close tags will be a subscript. EX: H2O
<br />
Adds a break in a paragraph when designated
<hr />
Adds a line between items to show a separation of themes
<strong>
Indacted as bold sometimes. Devices that speak to a user will change thier tone when coming to text encased in this tag
<em>
Indacted as italics sometimes. Devices that speak to a user will change thier tone when coming to text encased in this tag
<blockquote>
Used for quotes that would general take up a whole paragraph
<q>
Used to put quotations around a specific line of text. IE does not do this
<abbr>
Used to abbreviate long names or phrases, as well as, use anacronyms
<cite>
Used to reference someone elses work. Will show up in italics
<dfn>
Used to define a new term for the first time.
<address>
Will hold the information of the author of the current page
<ins>
Underlines a word that has been inserted into text that wasnt currnetly there
<del>
Places a line through a word of the original text that is being omitted
<s>
Strikes through a word of text that is no longer accurate. Displays same as <del>
