About the Town

The town has very deep backgrounds. Some families have at least 3 generations that have lived in the area. One family is the Williams. They happen to own more than have of the possible residential land in the area. Marvin William even owned the local sand mine before he passed away.

We have a few boat ramps and many houses with canal access that exits directly into the river. We have to recreational parks that can be used for events either with kids or to entertain adults. There is also the local cstrongetary should you decide you want to be buried in the area.

We also are home to the Meadowlark Campground. It's not a traditional campground. It's more of an RV/trailer park for 55 and older. It has a small hall that is home to bingo and other events that anyone is welcome to join. It also has an in-ground pool for swimming and 2-3 pool tables. The campground also has shuffle board and horseshoe tournaments from time to time.

Events

Childrens Program

The children's program has been around for a few decades now. It's purpose is to take kids after school and during summer and allow to do arts and crafts, as well as, interact with other children that may not live in the area. It is available when there is funding available and an over abundance of children in the area. The summer portion of the program is usually held regardless of the amount of kids as children need something to do over the summer.

There are also seasonal parties every year for Halloween and Christmas. The halloween party usually contains games usually found at a halloween party and prizes for contests based on costumes. The Christmas party usually holds has a Santa for the kids to ge their picture taken with. There gifts that will be given to the children also. The night usually holds a hay ride to finish things off with caroling.

Things To Do

Hunting

There are a few locations that can be used to hunt on. You do need to have a mstrongbership to those areas however. They are generally open year round, but you dont see anyone until peak season.

Camping

One of our parks is home to a large amount of land. There are pavillions there and lake Wobegon with some boardwalks to which you can even fish off of. It should be mentioned that there are wild dogs and bears in the area though.

Fishing

If you have a boat you can rent a dock either from the Meadowlark campground, someone willing to let you use thier dock or bring it out of the water each day. The river has a few accessible ramps to put your boat in. You can also fish at one of the parks off the docks. We have an array of fish that are possible to catch. We have Bass, Snook, Gar, Jack and many others.
